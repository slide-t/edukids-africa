About the Author

EduKids Africa was founded by Azeez Olajide Alimi, an education innovator passionate about making learning accessible for every child across Africa. With expertise in digital education, curriculum design, and interactive platforms, Kenny envisions a future where technology bridges the learning gap and inspires curiosity in young minds.

Our Roadmap

Our mission is to provide inclusive, high-quality digital learning experiences that empower children across Africa to build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Our vision is to be Africa’s most trusted hub for innovative learning, inspiring creativity, confidence, and lifelong knowledge in every child.

  • Launch interactive literacy modules for Grades 1–3.
  • Develop gamified numeracy lessons and quizzes.
  • Expand into STEM, arts, and creative problem-solving resources.
  • Provide teacher dashboards with performance analytics.
  • Foster community engagement through webinars and workshops.
