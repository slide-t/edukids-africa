EduKids Africa

About the Author

EduKids Africa was founded by Kenny Omokehinde, an education enthusiast passionate about making learning accessible to children across Africa. With years of experience in digital education and curriculum development, Kenny aims to create interactive and engaging learning platforms tailored to young learners.

Our Roadmap

Our mission is to provide accessible, high-quality digital learning content that empowers children across Africa to develop foundational skills in literacy, numeracy, and critical thinking.

Our vision is to become the leading platform for interactive and engaging education in Africa, fostering a love of learning and unlocking the potential of every child.

  • Launch interactive literacy modules for grades 1-3.
  • Develop numeracy games and quizzes for foundational skills.
  • Expand platform to include STEM and creative learning resources.
  • Introduce teacher dashboards and analytics for student tracking.
  • Build community engagement through workshops and webinars.
